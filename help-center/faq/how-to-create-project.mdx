## Create Project

To create a new project in BG Ventures:

1. Go to the dashboard after logging in.
2. Click on **New Project** in the top right corner.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Click **Create** to finish setup.

Your new project will now appear on your dashboard.

```tip
You can invite team members from your project's settings page after creation.
```

---
title: Adding a New Project
description: Quick guide to start a project in BG Ventures.
---

## Step-by-Step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Projects">
    Click **Projects** in the left sidebar menu after logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Press the **New Project** button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Project Details">
    Provide a name and short description for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish and Open">
    Click **Create**. Your project dashboard opens instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

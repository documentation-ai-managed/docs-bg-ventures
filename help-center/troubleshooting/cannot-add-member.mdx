---
title: Cannot Add New Member
description: Troubleshooting member invitation issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Invite button is disabled
- Error: "Invitation failed"

## Causes

- Invalid email format
- User already invited
- Exceeded member limit

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email address">
    Make sure the email address is correct and not already used.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    Reload the dashboard to update project status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check member limit">
    Delete unused members or upgrade your plan if you reached the limit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
